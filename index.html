Transcript Alignment with Timestamps for Video Segmentation

This project focuses on aligning speech-to-text data with timestamps for video segmentation using the MMSum dataset. It employs WhisperX for speech extraction and RoBERTa for text segmentation and timestamp generation. The methodology combines Whisper, spaCy, Sentence Transformer, LDA topic modeling, and cosine similarity for dynamic text clustering. This approach ensures accurate speech-text alignment, enabling effective video summarization and segmentation, showcasing my skills in applying advanced NLP techniques to multimedia content..
